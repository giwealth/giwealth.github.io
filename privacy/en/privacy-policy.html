Privacy Policy

StockTaking Privacy Policy

StockTaking respects every user and discloses the use of sensitive permissions and personal data. By using StockTaking app, you, the user, consent to this policy.
All data collected is used on the device and is never accessed from anywhere else. To ensure this, StockTaking only records APP startup records.

Sensitive Permissions

  • Camera: Used to identify barcodes and QR codes.
  • Storage: Used to save the recognized barcode or QR code data.
